BABY TEETH

14.03 - 18.03

BABY TEETH showcases artwork from a collective of ten Goldsmiths artists reflecting on childhood and nostalgia. From the wonderment of childhood, to the complexity of finding meaning in young adulthood, multi-media exhibition BABY TEETH attempts to capture what it means to remember.

The show will be taking place on the ground floor of the St James Hatcham Building (The Church), Goldsmiths University of London.
